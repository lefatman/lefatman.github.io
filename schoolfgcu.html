Field Report

When many people think of the Field of computing and technology they tend to think of three main profession, those being namely; Computer Scientist , Software Engineer and ICT Manager. In fact many people see these professions as analogous (though nothing could be further from the truth). Computer science is the research and study of the theory, experimentation, and engineering that form the basis of External (non-neural) computing. It is the Empirical study of non-neural calculation, logic and its applications as well as the methodical study of its feasibility, structure, mode of expression, and implementation of the systematic instructions (or algorithms) that underlie the acquisition, representation, processing, storage, communication of, and access to information.The basis of computational theory truly precedes the innovation of the modern Digital CPU . It was first thought that Charles Babbage (inventor of the Analytical Engine) was the father of Computer Science with Ada Lovelace as the first Computer Programmer.This belief held for almost centuries until the discovery of the Antikythera Mechanism which theoretically predates the previously earliest known computational device of this type by over a thousand years. Though Highly need Computer Science as a field is starting to show signs of saturation. Software application Engineering is the application of engineering methods to programming and the development of software.When the first digital computer systems appeared in the early-mid 20th century,their programs were physically integrated into their systems. Therefore the abstraction of "software" from “hardware” started with said abstraction being used to handle the intricacy of computing.Software Engineers are here today because of the low quality software being produced at the time. The discipline being more or less the solution to all that bogged the software industry down. Projects within time and projected cost; guaranteeing that software is built methodically, rigorously,on time, and on budget. Engineering as a whole is a discipline meant to mitigate these very things. Thus the same concepts used in engineering can be used in software development . While Software Engineers tend focus on the non-physical aspects of Computing, ICT Managers are heavily focused on the execution of efficient software systems. Information and communications technology (ICT) is an another way to refer to information Technology (IT) which stresses the function of the unified interactions and the melding of telecoms ( ethernet and wifi ) and computer systems with needed business applications , middleware, storage, and audio-visual systems, which enable users to gain access to, store, transfer, and manipulate information.Despite the power of computer systems to enhance and reform educational practices, inappropriate implementation is an extensive problem beyond the reach of increased funding and technological advances with little proof that teachers and tutors are effectively incorporating ICT into daily scholastic life. Underlying barriers such as a belief in more conventional education, archaic attitudes towards computer systems in education as well as the Educator’s own feelings towards computers combined with their ability to utilize them ;all result in diminished effectiveness in the integration of ICT in the classroom. Out of the three disciplines ICT Seems to be the one with the most viability as it has shown any stagnation since it’s development. This all comes down to the simple fact that each of these disciplines is unique , with little true overlap in functionality.
